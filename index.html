How the Senate Works

An Architect proposes. A Skeptic from a different provider attacks. An Adversary from a third stress-tests. A Synthesizer resolves. An Arbiter scores.

Four independent training distributions mean a blind spot in one is caught by another. This runs before significant build decisions are committed. The output is not consensus, it is a decision that survived being attacked from four directions.

Why four providers

Every major model family has characteristic blind spots shaped by its training data. A decision that passes Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, and xAI adversarial review is structurally harder to get wrong than one reviewed by a single model, no matter how capable.

What Sovereign Deployment Means

Systems run on your hardware. Routine work is handled by a local model on-premise. Only structurally safe, de-identified abstractions ever reach a global model.

In the Regulated edition, protected data physically cannot reach any outside channel at all. When a global model went dark in mid-2026, sovereign systems kept running. That is the point.

Practically

A Mac mini or equivalent hardware at your location runs the local model. Your agents run there. Your data stays there. The only outbound traffic is structurally PHI-free questions and schemas, not records.

HIPAA by Design, Not by Filter

A de-identification filter is a tool making judgment calls, and a tool can fail and must be defended in an audit. Structural isolation cannot fail the same way.

Two zones: a Sealed Zone that holds protected data and never speaks outward, and an Open Zone that never receives protected data by construction. One wall, one code-enforced interface, one thing to prove. You point an auditor at the architecture, not at a log of filter decisions.

The Builder, Not the Deliverable

Tribune learns the shape of a problem, never a single record. That is why its knowledge is safely portable across clients and why each build makes the next one faster, without ever moving one client's data to another.

The first healthcare back-office encodes how the healthcare category works at the operational level. That structural knowledge transfers to the second healthcare client at no additional discovery cost. Healthcare teaches legal. Legal teaches finance.
